Best Newsletter Tools for 2025: Top Email Marketing Platforms Compared (Pricing, Features & Reviews)

Email marketing remains one of the highest-ROI channels for businesses of all sizes. Whether you're a solopreneur, a growing startup, or an established enterprise, choosing the right newsletter tools can make or break your email campaigns. With dozens of platforms on the market, finding the perfect fit requires comparing pricing, automation features, deliverability, and ease of use.

In this comprehensive guide, I'll walk you through the top newsletter tools for 2025, including detailed breakdowns of pricing, features, pros/cons, and my honest recommendations. By the end, you'll know exactly which platform aligns with your goals and budget.

1. Mailchimp – Best All-Rounder for Beginners & Small Businesses

Mailchimp is arguably the most recognized name in email marketing. It offers a generous free plan, a drag-and-drop builder, and powerful automation—making it a go-to choice for startups and small teams.

Pricing

  • Free Plan: Up to 500 contacts, 1,000 sends/month, basic templates, and one-user seat.
  • Essentials: Starts at $13/month (500 contacts). Includes A/B testing, custom branding, and 3 seats.
  • Standard: Starts at $20/month (500 contacts). Adds automation, retargeting, and advanced analytics.
  • Premium: Starts at $350/month (10,000+ contacts). Includes advanced segmentation, phone support, and custom reporting.

Key Features

  • Drag-and-drop email builder with 100+ templates
  • Marketing CRM with contact scoring
  • Customer journey builder for automation
  • Built-in social media ad integrations
  • A/B testing for subject lines, content, and send times

Pros

  • Extremely user-friendly interface
  • Generous free tier for small lists
  • Robust analytics and reporting
  • Large template library

Cons

  • Pricing jumps significantly as your list grows
  • Limited automation on lower tiers
  • Some users report deliverability issues with high-volume sends

Best For

Beginners, bloggers, and small e-commerce stores looking for an affordable entry point with room to scale.

2. ConvertKit – Best for Creators & Bloggers

ConvertKit is built specifically for creators, authors, and online educators. It focuses on simplicity, subscriber tagging, and landing pages—making it one of the best newsletter tools for building audience relationships.

Pricing

  • Free Plan: Up to 1,000 subscribers, unlimited landing pages, and email broadcasts.
  • Creator: Starts at $29/month (1,000 subscribers). Adds automation, subscriber scoring, and premium support.
  • Creator Pro: Starts at $59/month (1,000 subscribers). Includes advanced analytics, newsletter referral system, and deliverability reports.

Key Features

  • Visual automation builder with triggers and actions
  • Subscriber tagging and custom fields
  • Landing pages and forms (unlimited on free plan)
  • Newsletter referral system to grow your list
  • Integrations with WordPress, Shopify, and Stripe

Pros

  • Excellent for audience segmentation
  • Clean, distraction-free email editor
  • Strong deliverability reputation
  • Free plan supports up to 1,000 subscribers

Cons

  • No A/B testing on lower tiers
  • Limited template customization (mostly plain text)
  • Can become expensive for large lists

Best For

Bloggers, podcasters, course creators, and anyone who wants to build a loyal subscriber base with minimal fuss.

3. Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) – Best for Transactional Emails & SMS

Brevo combines email marketing with SMS, transactional emails, and a built-in CRM. It's a strong contender for businesses that need multichannel outreach without breaking the bank.

Pricing

  • Free Plan: 300 emails/day (unlimited contacts). Includes basic templates and transactional emails.
  • Starter: Starts at $25/month (20,000 emails/month). Removes daily limit and adds A/B testing.
  • Business: Starts at $65/month (20,000 emails/month). Adds automation, A/B testing, and phone support.
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for high-volume senders.

Key Features

  • Transactional email API (ideal for e-commerce confirmations)
  • SMS marketing campaigns
  • Drag-and-drop email builder
  • Marketing automation workflows
  • Built-in CRM for contact management

Pros

  • Very affordable for high-volume senders
  • Unlimited contacts on free plan (capped by daily sends)
  • Multichannel (email + SMS + chat)
  • Strong transactional email capabilities

Cons

  • Free plan limits daily send volume
  • Email editor less intuitive than Mailchimp or ConvertKit
  • Automation features locked behind higher tiers

Best For

E-commerce stores needing transactional emails, small businesses wanting SMS integration, and budget-conscious marketers.

4. ActiveCampaign – Best for Advanced Automation & CRM

ActiveCampaign is a powerhouse for marketing automation and CRM. It's ideal for businesses that want sophisticated email sequences, lead scoring, and deep customer insights.

Pricing

  • Starter: Starts at $15/month (1,000 contacts). Includes email campaigns, forms, and basic automation.
  • Plus: Starts at $49/month (1,000 contacts). Adds landing pages, lead scoring, and conditional content.
  • Professional: Starts at $79/month (1,000 contacts). Includes predictive sending, split automations, and attribution.
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for unlimited users and custom reporting.

Key Features

  • Visual automation builder with 500+ pre-built recipes
  • Built-in CRM with deal tracking
  • Lead scoring and predictive content
  • Conditional content for personalized emails
  • Split automations for A/B testing

Pros

  • Industry-leading automation capabilities
  • Deep CRM integration for sales teams
  • Excellent segmentation and dynamic content
  • Great deliverability rates

Cons

  • Steep learning curve for beginners
  • Higher cost for advanced features
  • Email builder can feel cluttered

Best For

Growing businesses, B2B companies, and marketers who need complex automation workflows and sales CRM integration.

5. MailerLite – Best Budget Option with Surprising Power

MailerLite offers a clean interface, strong features, and one of the most affordable pricing structures—making it a hidden gem among newsletter tools.

Pricing

  • Free Plan: Up to 1,000 subscribers, 12,000 emails/month (daily limit of 500).

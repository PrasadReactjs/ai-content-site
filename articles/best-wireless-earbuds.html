Key Features

  • Industry-leading Active Noise Cancellation (ANC)
  • Custom 8.4mm driver for rich, detailed sound
  • Up to 8 hours battery (24 hours with charging case)
  • IPX4 water resistance

Pros

  • Exceptional sound clarity with deep bass
  • Best-in-class ANC that blocks almost all ambient noise
  • Comfortable, compact design fits most ear shapes

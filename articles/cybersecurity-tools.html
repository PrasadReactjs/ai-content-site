Why You Need a VPN as a Core Cybersecurity Tool

Before diving into specific services, it's important to understand why a VPN belongs in your cybersecurity tools arsenal. A VPN (Virtual Private Network) encrypts your internet traffic, hides your IP address, and creates a secure tunnel between your device and the web. This prevents hackers, ISPs, and even governments from snooping on your online activities.

Modern VPNs go beyond basic encryption. They include features like kill switches, DNS leak protection, split tunneling, and ad-blocking. When you choose the best VPN service, you’re investing in privacy, freedom, and peace of mind.

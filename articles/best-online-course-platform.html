The Ultimate Guide to Finding the Best Online Course Platform in 2025

Are you ready to turn your expertise into income or finally master a new skill from the comfort of your home? The e-learning industry is booming, and choosing the best online course platform can make or break your success—whether you are a creator looking to sell courses or a student seeking top-tier education.

With hundreds of platforms available, each boasting unique features, pricing structures, and target audiences, finding the perfect fit can feel overwhelming. That’s why we’ve done the heavy lifting for you. In this comprehensive guide, we’ll break down the top contenders, compare their pricing and features, and help you decide which is truly the best online course platform for your specific needs.

What Makes the Best Online Course Platform?

Before we dive into the reviews, it’s essential to understand what criteria matter most. The best online course platform should offer:

  • Ease of use: A drag-and-drop builder or intuitive interface saves time.
  • Monetization options: Support for one-time payments, subscriptions, or bundles.
  • Marketing tools: Email integration, sales funnels, and SEO capabilities.
  • Student engagement: Quizzes, certificates, discussion forums, and drip content.
  • Scalability: Ability to grow from a single course to a full academy.
  • Pricing transparency: No hidden fees or surprise transaction costs.

Now, let’s explore the top platforms that consistently rank as the best online course platform for creators and learners alike.

1. Teachable – Best for All-in-One Course Creation

Teachable is a household name in the e-learning space, and for good reason. It offers a robust, all-in-one solution for creators who want to build, market, and sell courses without needing technical skills.

Pricing

  • Free Plan: $0/month + 5% transaction fee per sale.
  • Basic Plan: $39/month (billed annually) – 5% transaction fee.
  • Pro Plan: $119/month (billed annually) – 0% transaction fee.
  • Business Plan: $299/month (billed annually) – 0% transaction fee.

Features

  • Drag-and-drop course builder with multimedia support (video, PDF, quizzes).
  • Built-in sales pages and checkout.
  • Email marketing integrations (Mailchimp, ConvertKit).
  • Drip content scheduling and certificates of completion.
  • Native affiliate program management.

Pros

  • Extremely beginner-friendly with no coding required.
  • Reliable hosting and fast video streaming.
  • Strong community and support resources.

Cons

  • Transaction fees on lower-tier plans can eat into profits.
  • Limited design customization without CSS knowledge.
  • No built-in blog or website builder (requires third-party integration).

Recommendation: Teachable is the best online course platform for solo creators and small businesses who want a simple, reliable way to sell courses without worrying about technical maintenance.

2. Thinkific – Best for Customization and Free Plan

Thinkific is a direct competitor to Teachable and often praised for its generous free plan and flexible customization options. It’s a top contender for the best online course platform among entrepreneurs who want more control over their branding.

Pricing

  • Free Plan: $0/month – unlimited students, 1 course, 0% transaction fees.
  • Basic Plan: $49/month (billed annually) – 3 courses, 0% transaction fees.
  • Pro Plan: $119/month (billed annually) – unlimited courses, 0% fees.
  • Premier Plan: $499/month (billed annually) – advanced features.

Features

  • Fully customizable course player and website themes.
  • Built-in community features (discussions, groups).
  • Advanced quiz options (timed, graded, surveys).
  • Direct integrations with Zoom for live lessons.
  • Automated email sequences and coupons.

Pros

  • No transaction fees on any plan, including free.
  • Excellent for building a branded learning experience.
  • Strong analytics and reporting dashboard.

Cons

  • Limited design flexibility compared to website builders like WordPress.
  • Customer support can be slow on free plan.
  • Advanced features (like mobile app) require higher-tier plans.

Recommendation: Thinkific is the best online course platform for creators who want to start for free and later scale without worrying about transaction fees eating into revenue.

3. Kajabi – Best for All-in-One Business Platform

Kajabi is more than just a course platform—it’s a complete business ecosystem. It combines course creation, email marketing, landing pages, and sales funnels into one seamless tool. For serious entrepreneurs, Kajabi often claims the title of best online course platform due to its all-in-one nature.

Pricing

  • Basic Plan: $149/month (billed annually) – 3 products, 10,000 contacts.
  • Growth Plan: $199/month (billed annually) – 15 products, 25,000 contacts.
  • Pro Plan: $399/month (billed annually) – 100 products, 100,000 contacts.

Features

  • Built-in email marketing with automation pipelines.
  • Landing page builder with pre-designed templates.
  • Sales funnel creation (opt-in, tripwire, upsells).
  • Pipeline management for student progress tracking.
  • Mobile app for students (iOS/Android).

Pros

  • Eliminates the need for separate email and landing page tools.
  • High-conversion funnel templates included.
  • Excellent customer support and onboarding.

Cons

  • Higher price point—not ideal for beginners on a tight budget.
  • Steeper learning curve due to many features.
  • Limited course player customization compared to standalone platforms.

Recommendation: Kajabi is the best online course platform for established creators or coaches who want to consolidate their marketing and sales tools into one subscription.

4. LearnWorlds – Best for Interactive Learning

LearnWorlds stands out for its focus on student engagement and interactive learning experiences. If you want to create a truly immersive educational environment, this might be the best online course platform for you.

Pricing

  • Pro Trainer Plan: $99/month (billed annually) – 5 admins, unlimited courses.
  • Learning Center Plan: $299/month (billed annually) – 10 admins, custom branding.

Features

  • Interactive video (quizzes, clickable links within videos).
  • Built-in social learning community.
  • White-label mobile app (on higher plans).
  • SCORM & xAPI support for advanced assessments.
  • E-commerce capabilities (subscriptions, bundles, coupons).

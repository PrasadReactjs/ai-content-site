The Ultimate Guide to Finding the Best Online Course Platform for Your Teaching or Learning Goals

In 2025, the e-learning industry has exploded into a multi-billion dollar ecosystem, with thousands of instructors and millions of students seeking the best online course platform to create, market, and consume digital education. Whether you’re a seasoned educator looking to monetize your expertise or a lifelong learner hunting for high-quality courses, choosing the right platform can make or break your experience. This comprehensive guide compares the top contenders—Udemy, Teachable, Thinkific, Kajabi, and Coursera—by diving into pricing, features, pros/cons, and actionable recommendations.

By the end of this article, you’ll have a clear roadmap to select the best online course platform that aligns with your budget, technical skills, and long-term goals. Let’s get started.

Why the Right Platform Matters

Not all course platforms are created equal. Some prioritize ease of use for beginners, while others offer advanced marketing and automation tools for scaling a business. The best online course platform for a solo creator might be a poor fit for a corporate trainer or a university. Key factors include:

  • Pricing structure: Transaction fees vs. flat monthly subscriptions
  • Customization: Branding, design flexibility, and course layout
  • Marketing tools: Built-in audience, email integrations, and sales funnels
  • Student experience: Mobile app, community features, and certificates
  • Scalability: Ability to grow from a single course to a full academy

We’ve evaluated each platform against these criteria to help you make an informed decision.

Top 5 Best Online Course Platforms Compared

1. Udemy – Best for Reaching a Massive Built-In Audience

Overview: Udemy is the world’s largest marketplace for online courses, with over 60 million students. It’s ideal for instructors who want to focus on content creation without worrying about marketing or hosting.

Pricing

  • Free to join – No monthly subscription for instructors.
  • Revenue split: 97% instructor revenue for organic sales (via your coupon), 63% for Udemy-driven sales.
  • Students: Courses typically priced between $19.99 and $199.99, often discounted heavily.

Key Features

  • Built-in marketplace with millions of active users
  • Mobile app for students (iOS/Android)
  • Course Q&A, announcements, and review system
  • Automatic captioning and video hosting
  • Certificate of completion

Pros

  • Instant access to a huge student base—no marketing needed
  • No upfront costs or technical setup
  • Excellent for beginners testing the waters

Cons

  • Low control over pricing (Udemy often runs $10–$15 sales)
  • Limited branding – your course sits alongside competitors
  • No email marketing or advanced sales funnels
  • Revenue share is low on Udemy-promoted sales

Recommendation: Choose Udemy if you’re a new instructor wanting to validate a topic or build a student base quickly. It’s not the best online course platform for building a standalone brand or maximizing per-course profit.

2. Teachable – Best for Beginner Course Creators Who Want Full Control

Overview: Teachable is a hosted platform that lets you create and sell your own courses with a branded school. It’s user-friendly and offers robust features without requiring coding.

Pricing

  • Free plan: $0 + 5% transaction fee per sale
  • Basic plan: $39/month + 5% transaction fee
  • Pro plan: $119/month + 0% transaction fee
  • Business plan: $299/month + 0% transaction fee

Key Features

  • Drag-and-drop course builder with quizzes and certificates
  • Custom domain and full branding
  • Integrated payment processing (Stripe, PayPal)
  • Email marketing (basic) and coupon codes
  • Student management and analytics
  • Native iOS app for students (Pro plan and above)

Pros

  • Very intuitive interface—no technical skills required
  • Unlimited courses and students on paid plans
  • Good for bundling courses and offering memberships
  • Strong customer support

Cons

  • Transaction fees on lower tiers can eat profits
  • Limited advanced marketing (no built-in affiliate system on Basic)
  • No built-in course marketplace (you must drive your own traffic)

Recommendation: Teachable is the best online course platform for solo creators and small businesses who want a professional-looking school without a steep learning curve. Upgrade to Pro to eliminate transaction fees.

3. Thinkific – Best for Customization and Scalability

Overview: Thinkific is a powerful all-in-one platform that offers more design flexibility and advanced features than Teachable, making it a favorite among tech-savvy creators.

Pricing

  • Free plan: $0 with 0% transaction fees (limited to 3 courses and basic features)
  • Basic plan: $49/month (unlimited courses, 0% fees)
  • Pro plan: $99/month (adds certificates, assignments, and community)
  • Premier plan: $199/month (advanced analytics and onboarding)

Key Features

  • Customizable course player and site builder
  • Built-in community (Pro plan and above)
  • Advanced quizzes, surveys, and assignments
  • Direct integrations with Mailchimp, ConvertKit, and Zapier
  • Membership and subscription options
  • Mobile-responsive design

Pros

  • No transaction fees on any paid plan
  • Rich customization options (CSS/HTML access)
  • Excellent for cohort-based courses and live sessions
  • Strong API and third-party integrations

Cons

  • Steeper learning curve for beginners
  • Free plan is quite limited (3 courses, no certificates)
  • Community feature is basic compared to dedicated tools

Recommendation: If you’re looking for the best online course platform that balances customization with ease of use, Thinkific is a top contender. It’s ideal for creators who want to build a scalable academy with zero transaction fees.

4. Kajabi – Best All-in-One Business Platform

Overview: Kajabi is not just a course platform—it’s a complete business solution with built-in email marketing, sales funnels, and analytics. It’s designed for serious entrepreneurs.

Pricing

  • Basic plan: $149/month (3 products, 1,000 active members)
  • Growth plan: $199/month (15 products, 10,000 active members)
  • Pro plan: $399/month (100 products, 20,000 active members)
  • No transaction fees on any plan

Key Features

  • All-in-one: course builder, email marketing, pipelines, and landing pages
  • Automated marketing sequences (broadcasts, drip campaigns)
  • Built-in affiliate program management

Best Email Marketing Software for Marketing Automation in 2025: Pricing, Features & Recommendations

Email marketing is far from dead—it's evolving. In fact, with the rise of marketing automation, email remains one of the highest-ROI channels for businesses of all sizes. But with dozens of platforms vying for your attention, how do you choose the right one? In this comprehensive guide, we'll break down the best email marketing tools for marketing automation, comparing pricing, features, pros, and cons to help you make an informed decision.

Whether you're a solopreneur, a growing startup, or an established enterprise, the right marketing automation platform can save you hours, boost conversions, and nurture leads on autopilot. Let's dive in.

What Is Marketing Automation in Email Marketing?

Before we compare tools, let's clarify what marketing automation means in this context. It's the use of software to automate repetitive marketing tasks—like sending welcome emails, abandoned cart reminders, or segmented campaigns—based on user behavior, triggers, or schedules. The goal is to deliver the right message to the right person at the right time, without manual effort.

The best email marketing platforms for marketing automation offer features like:

  • Visual workflow builders (drag-and-drop automation)
  • Behavioral triggers (e.g., email opens, link clicks, purchases)
  • Dynamic content and personalization
  • Lead scoring and segmentation
  • A/B testing and analytics

Now, let's explore the top contenders.

1. ActiveCampaign – Best All-in-One Marketing Automation Platform

ActiveCampaign is widely regarded as the gold standard for marketing automation. It combines email marketing, automation, CRM, and sales tools in one platform. It's ideal for small to medium businesses that want advanced automation without enterprise-level complexity.

Pricing

  • Starter: $15/month (up to 1,000 contacts) – basic email and automation
  • Plus: $49/month – adds landing pages, lead scoring, and conditional content
  • Professional: $79/month – includes predictive sending, split automations, and attribution
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing – advanced security and custom reporting

Key Features

  • Visual automation builder with over 500 pre-built templates
  • Conditional content and dynamic segmentation
  • Built-in CRM with deal tracking
  • Predictive sending and machine learning
  • Deep integrations (Shopify, WordPress, Salesforce, etc.)

Pros

  • Extremely powerful automation logic
  • Excellent deliverability rates
  • User-friendly drag-and-drop interface
  • Great value for advanced features

Cons

  • Steep learning curve for beginners
  • Email templates could be more modern
  • Some users report slow customer support response times

Recommendation

Best for businesses that need robust marketing automation and are willing to invest time in setup. If you want to create complex, behavior-driven campaigns, ActiveCampaign is our top pick.

2. Mailchimp – Best for Beginners and Small Teams

Mailchimp is one of the most recognizable names in email marketing. Its recent updates have significantly improved its marketing automation capabilities, making it more competitive with specialized tools.

Pricing

  • Free: Up to 500 contacts with limited automation (basic triggers only)
  • Essentials: $13/month (500 contacts) – adds 3 automation journeys
  • Standard: $20/month – unlimited automations, A/B testing, and segmentation
  • Premium: $350/month – advanced segmentation, multivariate testing, and phone support

Key Features

  • Customer Journey Builder with branching logic
  • Predictive segmentation and send-time optimization
  • Built-in CRM and audience dashboard
  • Creative Assistant (AI-generated designs)
  • Integrated landing pages and forms

Pros

  • Intuitive interface – great for beginners
  • Generous free plan
  • Strong template library
  • Excellent analytics and reporting

Cons

  • Automation is still less advanced than ActiveCampaign or HubSpot
  • Pricing can get expensive as your list grows
  • Some advanced features are locked behind higher tiers

Recommendation

Ideal for startups and freelancers who want a simple, all-in-one solution. If you're just getting started with marketing automation, Mailchimp's free plan is a great way to test the waters.

3. HubSpot Marketing Hub – Best for Enterprise and CRM Integration

HubSpot is a powerhouse for inbound marketing. Its Marketing Hub offers deep marketing automation capabilities tightly integrated with its free CRM. It's designed for businesses that need a complete suite—from email to social media to analytics.

Pricing

  • Free: Limited email sends (2,000/month), basic automation
  • Starter: $20/month (1,000 contacts) – adds email marketing and forms
  • Professional: $800/month (2,000 contacts) – full automation, A/B testing, smart content
  • Enterprise: $3,600/month – predictive lead scoring, custom reporting, multi-touch attribution

Key Features

  • Visual automation workflows with conditional branching
  • Smart content (personalization based on lifecycle stage)
  • Lead scoring and routing
  • Conversational bots and live chat
  • Comprehensive analytics and attribution reporting

Pros

  • Seamless integration with HubSpot CRM
  • Very powerful for B2B lead nurturing
  • Excellent support and educational resources
  • Scalable from small to enterprise

Cons

  • Expensive at Professional and Enterprise tiers
  • Can be overwhelming for beginners
  • Email design tools are less flexible than some competitors

Recommendation

Best for B2B companies and growing businesses that already use HubSpot CRM. If you need a unified platform for sales and marketing automation, HubSpot is unmatched—but be prepared for the price jump.

4. GetResponse – Best for Webinars and Funnel Building

GetResponse is a versatile platform that goes beyond email. It includes webinar hosting, landing page builders, and a conversion funnel feature—all powered by marketing automation.

Pricing

  • Free: Up to 500 contacts with basic automation (limited)
  • Email Marketing: $15/month (1,000 contacts) – standard automation
  • Marketing Automation: $49/month – advanced automation, funnels, webinars
  • Ecommerce Marketing: $99/month – product recommendations, abandoned cart

Key Features

  • Visual automation builder with time-based and behavior triggers
  • Built-in webinar platform (up to 1,000 attendees)
  • Conversion funnels with templates
  • AI-powered subject line generator
  • Dynamic content and segmentation

Pros

  • Unique webinar integration for lead

Chart Types You Can Create

A good chart generator offers variety. Here are the most common chart types and when to use them:

📊 Bar & Column Charts

Ideal for comparing quantities across categories. Use horizontal bars when category names are long, and vertical columns when you want to emphasize ranking over time.

📈 Line & Area Charts

Perfect for showing trends over time — sales growth, website traffic, temperature changes. Area charts add a filled effect that emphasizes magnitude.

The .io extension has become synonymous with technology, startups, and blockchain innovation. Originally assigned to the British Indian Ocean Territory, .io has been repurposed by the tech community as an abbreviation for "input/output" and is now a preferred choice for crypto, fintech, and SaaS companies. BlockVault.io combines this powerful TLD with a highly descriptive and memorable second-level domain.

  • Brandability: "BlockVault" immediately conveys security, storage, and blockchain technology. It’s short, punchy, and easy to recall.

Best Online Course Platforms: A Complete Guide to Course Creation in 2025

Are you ready to turn your expertise into a thriving online business? Whether you’re a coach, educator, or entrepreneur, choosing the right platform for course creation is the most critical decision you’ll make. The global e-learning market is booming, and with the right tools, you can build, market, and sell courses that generate passive income for years to come.

In this comprehensive guide, we’ll review the top online course platforms, comparing pricing, features, pros/cons, and real-world recommendations. By the end, you’ll know exactly which platform fits your course creation goals—and how to launch your first course with confidence.

What to Look for in a Course Creation Platform

Before diving into specific platforms, let’s outline the essential features every course creation tool should offer:

  • Ease of use – Drag-and-drop builders, intuitive dashboards, and no coding required.
  • Multimedia support – Upload videos, PDFs, quizzes, and downloadable resources.
  • Marketing tools – Email integration, sales pages, coupons, and affiliate programs.
  • Payment processing – Secure checkout, multiple currencies, and tax handling.
  • Scalability – Ability to grow from a single course to a full membership site.
  • Customer support – Reliable help via chat, email, or knowledge base.

Now, let’s explore the best platforms for course creation in 2025.

1. Teachable – Best for All-in-One Course Creation

Pricing

  • Free plan: $0 (transaction fee of 5% + $1 per sale)
  • Basic plan: $39/month (5% transaction fee)
  • Pro plan: $119/month (0% transaction fee)
  • Business plan: $299/month (custom features)

Key Features

  • Drag-and-drop course builder with multimedia lectures
  • Built-in sales pages and checkout
  • Email marketing integration (Mailchimp, ConvertKit)
  • Quizzes, certificates, and student analytics
  • Affiliate management system

Pros

  • Extremely user-friendly for beginners in course creation
  • No technical skills required to launch
  • Excellent customer support and community
  • Mobile-friendly for students

Cons

  • Transaction fees on lower-tier plans can eat into profits
  • Limited customization for advanced branding
  • No built-in community features (like forums)

Recommendation

Teachable is ideal for first-time course creators who want a simple, reliable platform. If you’re focused on course creation without worrying about technical headaches, start with the free plan and upgrade as you grow.

2. Thinkific – Best for Customization and Scalability

Pricing

  • Free plan: $0 (unlimited courses, but limited students)
  • Basic plan: $49/month (unlimited students)
  • Pro plan: $99/month (advanced features)
  • Premier plan: $199/month (custom branding)

Key Features

  • Fully customizable course templates
  • Built-in community (discussions, groups)
  • Quizzes, surveys, and assignments
  • Direct integration with Zoom for live sessions
  • Advanced analytics and reporting

Pros

  • No transaction fees on any paid plan
  • Powerful customization for branding and design
  • Strong community features to engage students
  • Excellent for scaling with memberships and bundles

Cons

  • Learning curve for advanced customization
  • Free plan limits students to 100
  • Email marketing tools are basic compared to dedicated platforms

Recommendation

Thinkific is perfect for creators who want control over their course creation process and plan to build a community. It’s a top choice for coaches and membership site owners.

3. Kajabi – Best for All-in-One Business Platform

Pricing

  • Basic plan: $149/month (3 products, 1,000 active members)
  • Growth plan: $199/month (15 products, 10,000 members)
  • Pro plan: $399/month (100 products, 20,000 members)

Key Features

  • Course creation with video hosting and drip content
  • Built-in email marketing and automation
  • Sales funnels, landing pages, and checkout
  • Community and membership site builder
  • Pipeline analytics and A/B testing

Pros

  • All-in-one platform (no need for separate tools)
  • Powerful marketing automation for course creation
  • Professional-looking sales funnels
  • Excellent customer support and training

Cons

  • Higher price point – not budget-friendly for beginners
  • Steep learning curve due to many features
  • Limited course design flexibility

Recommendation

Kajabi is best for established entrepreneurs who want a complete business solution. If you’re serious about course creation and have the budget, Kajabi streamlines everything from marketing to sales.

4. LearnWorlds – Best for Interactive Course Creation

Pricing

  • Starter plan: $29/month (transaction fee of $5 per sale)
  • Pro Trainer plan: $99/month (no transaction fees)
  • Learning Center plan: $299/month (custom branding)

Key Features

  • Interactive video with quizzes and annotations
  • White-label mobile app builder
  • Built-in social learning community
  • Advanced assessments and certifications
  • E-commerce integration (multiple payment gateways)

Pros

  • Best for creating engaging, interactive courses
  • Mobile app for students (iOS/Android)
  • No transaction fees on higher plans
  • Great for corporate training and academic use

Cons

  • Interface can be complex for beginners
  • Limited free plan (only 5 courses)
  • Customer support response times can vary

Recommendation

LearnWorlds is ideal for creators who want to build highly interactive course creation experiences. It’s excellent for educators and trainers who prioritize student engagement.

5. Podia – Best for Simple Course Creation and Digital Products

Pricing

  • Free plan: $0 (limited features, 10% transaction fee)
  • Mover plan: $39/month (0% transaction fee)
  • Shaker plan: $89/month (advanced features)
  • Earthquaker plan: $199/month (unlimited everything)

Key Features

  • Unlimited courses, downloads, and memberships
  • Built-in email marketing and sales pages
  • Drip content and quizzes
  • Live chat and customer support
  • Affiliate program for creators

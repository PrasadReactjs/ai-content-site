10 Best Landing Page Examples That Drive Sales: High Converting Pages in 2025

In the crowded digital marketplace, your landing page is often the only shot you have to turn a visitor into a customer. The difference between a bounce and a conversion often comes down to design, copy, and trust signals. We’ve analyzed hundreds of high converting pages to bring you this curated list of the best landing page examples.

Whether you're selling software, a physical product, or a service, these examples show you exactly what works. We’ll break down pricing, features, pros/cons, and give you actionable recommendations so you can replicate their success.

1. Unbounce – The OG Landing Page Builder

Best for: Marketers who want to test and optimize quickly.

Pricing

  • Launch Plan: $90/month (billed annually)
  • Optimize Plan: $135/month
  • Accelerate Plan: $225/month
  • Concierge Plan: Custom pricing

Features

  • Drag-and-drop builder with 100+ templates
  • AI-powered copywriting (Smart Copy)
  • A/B testing built-in
  • Dynamic text replacement for PPC
  • Integrates with 1000+ tools (Salesforce, HubSpot, Mailchimp)

Pros

  • Fast loading times out of the box
  • Excellent conversion tracking
  • Great for non-coders

Cons

  • Can get expensive at scale
  • Limited design flexibility compared to custom code

Recommendation: Unbounce is ideal if you run paid ads and need to test multiple variations of high converting pages weekly. Start with the Launch Plan.

2. Leadpages – Best for Beginners

Best for: Small businesses and solopreneurs on a budget.

Pricing

  • Standard Plan: $49/month (billed annually)
  • Pro Plan: $99/month
  • Advanced Plan: $149/month

Features

  • 200+ mobile-responsive templates
  • Built-in pop-ups and alert bars
  • Facebook and Instagram ad integration
  • Simple lead capture forms
  • Free custom domain with annual plan

Pros

  • Lowest price point for a professional tool
  • Easy to set up in under 30 minutes
  • Good customer support

Cons

  • Limited A/B testing (only on Pro plan)
  • Templates can feel generic

Recommendation: If you’re launching your first product and need a fast, affordable way to build high converting pages, Leadpages is your best bet. Upgrade to Pro for split testing.

3. ClickFunnels – All-in-One Sales Machine

Best for: Course creators and membership site owners.

Pricing

  • Basic Plan: $147/month
  • Pro Plan: $197/month
  • Funnel Hacker Plan: $297/month

Features

  • Complete sales funnel builder (landing pages, order forms, upsells)
  • Email marketing automation (Actionetics)
  • Membership site hosting
  • Affiliate center for referrals
  • Share funnels with other users

Pros

  • Everything in one platform – no need for extra tools
  • Pre-built funnels that convert
  • Excellent for high-ticket offers

Cons

  • Steep learning curve
  • Expensive compared to standalone landing page builders
  • Can be overkill for simple pages

Recommendation: Use ClickFunnels if you sell online courses or digital products and want to create complete sales journeys with high converting pages at every step.

4. Instapage – Enterprise-Grade Optimization

Best for: Large teams and agencies.

Pricing

  • Build Plan: $199/month (billed annually)
  • Convert Plan: $299/month
  • Custom Plan: Contact for pricing

Features

  • Collaboration tools (real-time editing, comments)
  • Heatmaps and session recordings
  • Global blocks for brand consistency
  • AdMap for visualizing ad-to-landing page journeys
  • AMP pages for lightning-fast mobile loading

Pros

  • Best-in-class collaboration features
  • Advanced analytics and personalization
  • Enterprise-grade security

Cons

  • Expensive for individuals
  • Overwhelming number of features for small businesses

Recommendation: Instapage is the gold standard for teams that need to produce high converting pages at scale. If you have a marketing team of 5+ people, this is worth the investment.

5. GetResponse – Email + Landing Pages Combo

Best for: Email marketers who want landing pages included.

Pricing

  • Email Marketing Plan: $19/month (1,000 contacts)
  • Marketing Automation Plan: $59/month
  • Ecommerce Marketing Plan: $119/month

Features

  • Landing page builder with 150+ templates
  • Email marketing and automation
  • Webinar hosting
  • Conversion funnel builder
  • AI email generator

Pros

  • Very affordable for combined email + landing pages
  • Good for list building campaigns
  • Simple interface

Cons

  • Landing page builder is less powerful than dedicated tools
  • Limited template variety

Recommendation: If you already use GetResponse for email, their landing page builder is a solid addition for creating high converting pages without extra cost.

6. Landingi – Budget-Friendly with AI

Best for: Freelancers and small agencies.

Pricing

  • Core Plan: $59/month
  • Create Plan: $89/month
  • Automate Plan: $149/month

Features

  • AI landing page generator (Lander AI)
  • 300+ templates
  • A/B testing on all plans
  • Pop-up and form builder
  • Integrations with Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Zapier

Pros

  • AI can generate a landing page in 30 seconds
  • Affordable for the features offered
  • Good for quick campaign launches

Cons

  • AI-generated pages need human editing
  • Customer support can be slow

Recommendation: Landingi is perfect for testing multiple ideas fast. Use

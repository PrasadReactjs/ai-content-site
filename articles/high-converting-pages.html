7 High Converting Pages That Crush It: Best Landing Page Examples With Pricing & Features

If you’re looking to boost your conversion rates, you’ve landed in the right place. A well-designed landing page can be the difference between a casual visitor and a paying customer. In this article, we break down the best landing page examples that are proven to generate high converting pages. We’ll cover pricing, features, pros/cons, and actionable tips so you can replicate their success.

Whether you’re a marketer, entrepreneur, or freelancer, these examples will show you exactly what makes a landing page convert. Let’s dive in.

What Makes a Landing Page High Converting?

Before we look at examples, let’s set the foundation. A high converting page typically includes:

  • Clear value proposition – The first thing a visitor sees should answer “What’s in it for me?”
  • Strong call-to-action (CTA) – One primary action, no distractions.
  • Social proof – Testimonials, reviews, or case studies.
  • Minimal friction – Short forms, fast loading, and mobile-friendly design.
  • Visual hierarchy – Headlines, images, and buttons guide the eye.

Now, let’s explore real-world examples that nail these elements.

1. Unbounce – The Conversion Intelligence Platform

Unbounce is a landing page builder that also practices what it preaches. Their own landing pages are masterclasses in conversion optimization.

Key Features

  • Drag-and-drop builder with 100+ templates
  • AI-powered copywriting assistant (Smart Copy)
  • A/B testing and dynamic text replacement
  • Popup and sticky bar builder
  • Integrates with major email marketing and CRM tools

Pricing

Starts at $99/month (Build plan), $149/month (Optimize plan), $249/month (Accelerate plan). 14-day free trial available.

Pros

  • Excellent template library for high converting pages
  • Powerful A/B testing capabilities
  • AI tool saves time on copywriting

Cons

  • Can be pricey for small businesses
  • Steeper learning curve for advanced features

Recommendation

Best for marketers and agencies who need robust testing and personalization. Use Unbounce if you want to run multiple experiments to optimize your high converting pages.

2. Leadpages – Simple Yet Effective Landing Pages

Leadpages is known for its simplicity and speed. Their own landing pages focus on one clear message and a single CTA, making them ideal for lead generation.

Key Features

  • 200+ mobile-responsive templates
  • Simple drag-and-drop editor
  • Built-in alert bars and pop-ups
  • Lead capture forms with automatic email follow-ups
  • Facebook and Instagram ad integration

Pricing

Starts at $49/month (Standard plan), $99/month (Pro plan), $199/month (Advanced plan). 14-day free trial.

Pros

  • Very easy to use – no coding needed
  • Affordable entry price
  • Fast page load times

Cons

  • Limited A/B testing (only on Pro plan)
  • Fewer customization options than Unbounce

Recommendation

Perfect for solopreneurs and small businesses who want to create high converting pages quickly without a big budget.

3. ClickFunnels – All-in-One Sales Funnel Builder

ClickFunnels doesn’t just build landing pages – it builds entire sales funnels. Their own landing pages are designed to move visitors from awareness to purchase in a seamless flow.

Key Features

  • Pre-built funnel templates (including opt-in, sales, and webinar funnels)
  • Email automation (Actionetics)
  • Order bumps and upsells built into pages
  • A/B split testing
  • Membership site hosting

Pricing

Starts at $147/month (Basic plan), $197/month (Pro plan), $297/month (Funnel Hacker plan). 14-day free trial.

Pros

  • Complete funnel solution – not just landing pages
  • Excellent for e-commerce and digital products
  • Strong community and training resources

Cons

  • Higher price point
  • Can feel complex for beginners

Recommendation

Ideal for businesses that sell courses, memberships, or physical products. If you want to build entire funnels with high converting pages, ClickFunnels is a top choice.

4. Instapage – Enterprise-Grade Landing Pages

Instapage focuses on post-click optimization, ensuring every visitor lands on a relevant, personalized page. Their own examples are clean, fast, and conversion-focused.

Key Features

  • 500+ customizable templates
  • Heatmaps for visual behavior analysis
  • Collaboration tools for teams (comments, approvals)
  • Dynamic text replacement for ad campaigns
  • AMP (Accelerated Mobile Pages) support

Pricing

Starts at $199/month (Build plan), $299/month (Convert plan), custom pricing for Enterprise. 14-day free trial.

Pros

  • Best-in-class post-click optimization
  • Heatmaps and A/B testing included
  • Great for large teams and agencies

Cons

  • Expensive for small businesses
  • Overkill if you only need basic landing pages

Recommendation

Best for enterprises and marketing teams that need advanced analytics and personalization to create high converting pages at scale.

5. GetResponse – Landing Pages + Email Marketing

GetResponse offers a landing page builder as part of a broader marketing platform. Their pages are designed to capture leads and nurture them via email.

Key Features

  • 150+ landing page templates
  • Built-in email marketing automation
  • Webinar hosting and funnel builder
  • A/B testing for pages and emails
  • Conversion analytics

Pricing

Starts at $19/month (Email Marketing plan), $59/month (Marketing Automation plan), $119/month (Ecommerce Marketing plan). 30-day free trial.

Pros

  • Very affordable – includes email marketing
  • All-in-one solution for small businesses
  • Easy to use with good support

Cons

  • Less advanced landing page features than dedicated tools
  • Template quality varies

Recommendation

Great for startups and small businesses that want both landing pages and email automation in one tool. It’s a budget-friendly way to create high converting pages.

6. HubSpot – Free Landing Pages With CRM Integration

HubSpot’s landing page builder is part of its free CRM and marketing tools. Their own pages are simple, clean, and optimized for lead capture.

Key Features

  • Free landing page builder (with HubSpot CRM)
  • Drag-and-drop editor with pre-built modules

Why GreenTech.io Commands Premium Value

The .io extension has become synonymous with tech startups, SaaS platforms, and innovative digital products. Originally assigned to the British Indian Ocean Territory, .io has been repurposed by the tech community as an abbreviation for "input/output," making it highly desirable for technology-focused brands. When combined with "GreenTech"—a term that represents the $10+ billion sustainable technology sector—the domain becomes a powerful branding tool.

Technical Audit: Domain Health and History

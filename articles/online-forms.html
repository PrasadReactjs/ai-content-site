The 7 Best Online Form Builders in 2024: Features, Pricing & Honest Reviews

Whether you're collecting leads, managing event RSVPs, conducting surveys, or processing payments, the right online forms tool can save you hours of manual work. But with dozens of form builders on the market, finding the perfect one for your needs can feel overwhelming.

In this comprehensive guide, I've tested and compared the top form builders based on ease of use, features, pricing, and real-world performance. By the end, you'll know exactly which platform is best for your specific use case.

What to Look for in an Online Form Builder

Before we dive into the reviews, here are the key factors to consider when choosing a form builder:

  • Drag-and-drop editor – How intuitive is the builder? Can you create forms without coding?
  • Integration options – Does it connect with your CRM, email marketing tools, and payment gateways?
  • Mobile responsiveness – Are your forms optimized for smartphones and tablets?
  • Conditional logic – Can you show or hide fields based on user responses?
  • Pricing – Is the cost reasonable for the features offered at your scale?

Now, let's explore the best online forms platforms available today.

1. JotForm – Best Overall for Versatility

Pricing: Free plan (5 forms, 100 submissions/month) | Paid plans start at $39/month (Bronze) to $99/month (Gold)

Key Features

  • 10,000+ customizable templates
  • Drag-and-drop form builder with widgets
  • Conditional logic for smart forms
  • 1,000+ integrations (PayPal, Stripe, Salesforce, Mailchimp)
  • File uploads up to 1 GB on paid plans
  • PDF form converter
  • HIPAA compliance (on Gold plan)

Pros

  • Massive template library saves time
  • Excellent free tier for beginners
  • Strong form analytics and reporting
  • Works well for complex, multi-page forms

Cons

  • Free plan limits submissions to 100/month
  • Some templates look slightly dated
  • Advanced features require higher-tier plans

Best for: Freelancers, small businesses, and anyone who needs a feature-rich form builder with tons of templates.

2. Typeform – Best for Engaging, Interactive Forms

Pricing: Free plan (10 forms, 10 responses/month) | Paid plans start at $25/month (Essentials) to $83/month (Professional)

Key Features

  • Conversational, one-question-at-a-time format
  • Beautiful design templates with custom branding
  • Logic jumps and calculations
  • Integrations with Slack, Google Sheets, HubSpot
  • Video and image backgrounds
  • Hidden fields and URL variables

Pros

  • Higher completion rates due to engaging format
  • Stunning, modern design out of the box
  • Great for quizzes, surveys, and lead gen

Cons

  • Free plan is very limited (only 10 responses/month)
  • Not ideal for long, data-heavy forms
  • Advanced logic can be tricky to set up

Best for: Marketers, content creators, and brands that prioritize user experience and design.

👉 Get Started with Typeform

  • File uploads and quiz mode
  • Collaboration with team members in real time

Pros

  • 100% free with no limits on submissions
  • Seamless integration with Google Workspace
  • Easy to share via link or embed

Cons

  • Limited customization and branding options
  • No advanced conditional logic
  • Basic design – not suitable for professional use
  • No payment collection

Best for: Teachers, students, internal surveys, and anyone on a tight budget.

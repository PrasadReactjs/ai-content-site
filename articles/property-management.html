Best CRM for Real Estate: Streamline Your Property Management in 2025

Managing a real estate business is no small feat. Between tracking leads, scheduling property showings, and handling client communications, it's easy to feel overwhelmed. That's where a dedicated Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system comes in—especially one tailored for property management. The right CRM can automate repetitive tasks, centralize client data, and help you close deals faster while keeping your rental or sales portfolio organized.

In this article, we'll dive into the best CRM for real estate professionals who need robust property management features. We'll cover pricing, pros and cons, and give you actionable recommendations to choose the perfect tool for your business.

Why Real Estate Professionals Need a CRM with Property Management Features

While generic CRMs exist, real estate has unique demands. You're not just tracking contacts—you're managing listings, lease agreements, maintenance requests, and commission splits. A CRM built for property management integrates these tasks into one dashboard, saving you hours each week.

Here’s what to look for in a real estate CRM:

  • Lead capture and automation – Automatically import leads from your website, Zillow, or Realtor.com.
  • Pipeline management – Visualize where each client or property is in the buying/selling/renting process.
  • Property tracking – Store property details, photos, documents, and status updates.
  • Task and calendar sync – Schedule showings, inspections, and follow-ups without double-booking.
  • Reporting and analytics – Measure your team's performance and track key metrics like conversion rates.

Now, let's explore the top contenders that excel in property management capabilities.

1. HubSpot CRM – Best for Scalability and Free Starter Features

HubSpot CRM is a powerhouse for real estate teams of any size. While it's not exclusively built for real estate, its free tier is incredibly generous, and its paid plans add advanced automation and reporting.

Key Features for Property Management

  • Unlimited contacts and deals (free version)
  • Email tracking and templates
  • Custom pipeline stages (e.g., "Lead," "Showing Scheduled," "Under Contract")
  • Meeting scheduling tool that syncs with Google Calendar
  • Integration with popular real estate tools like Zapier and Mailchimp

Pricing

  • Free: $0/month – core CRM features, 1 user
  • Starter: $20/month per user – automation, custom properties
  • Professional: $100/month per user – advanced reporting, sequences
  • Enterprise: $150/month per user – custom reporting, permissions

Pros

  • Excellent free version for solo agents
  • User-friendly interface
  • Strong third-party integrations

Cons

  • Limited built-in property management tools (needs add-ons)
  • Can become expensive as you scale users

Recommendation

Best for agents starting out or small teams who need a flexible, low-cost CRM. Use it with a property listing plugin to manage properties effectively.

2. RealtyJuggler – Best Affordable CRM with Built-In Property Management

RealtyJuggler is a budget-friendly option designed specifically for real estate agents. It includes robust property management features right out of the box, making it ideal for agents who handle both sales and rentals.

Key Features for Property Management

  • Property database with photo uploads and status tracking
  • Lease and tenant management (rent due dates, lease renewals)
  • Commission tracking and expense logging
  • Email and SMS integration
  • Mobile app for iOS and Android

Pricing

  • Starter: $29/month – 1 user, basic features
  • Professional: $59/month – unlimited users, advanced reporting
  • Enterprise: $99/month – custom branding, API access

Pros

  • Very affordable for the features offered
  • Native property management tools
  • No long-term contracts

Cons

  • Outdated user interface
  • Limited marketing automation

Recommendation

Perfect for independent agents or small brokerages that need property management without breaking the bank. The commission tracking is a standout feature.

3. AppFolio – Best for Large Property Management Firms

AppFolio is an enterprise-grade solution designed for professional property managers handling hundreds of units. It goes beyond CRM to include full accounting, maintenance, and tenant screening.

Key Features for Property Management

  • Online rent collection and payment processing
  • Maintenance request tracking with vendor management
  • Tenant screening and lease generation
  • Automated late fee calculations
  • Customizable dashboards for portfolio performance

Pricing

  • Core: Starts at $280/month – up to 200 units
  • Plus: Custom pricing – additional features like AI leasing assistant
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing – dedicated support

Pros

  • All-in-one solution for large portfolios
  • Excellent accounting and financial reporting
  • Tenant portal for self-service

Cons

  • Expensive for small teams
  • Steep learning curve

Recommendation

Ideal for property management companies with 50+ units. If you need serious property management automation, AppFolio is a top contender.

4. LionDesk – Best for Lead Generation and Follow-Up

LionDesk is a real estate CRM that focuses heavily on communication and lead nurturing. It's especially strong for agents who rely on drip campaigns and video messaging.

Key Features for Property Management

  • Automated text and email drip campaigns
  • Video email integration (personalized videos)
  • Property listing management with status updates
  • Call tracking and recording
  • Transaction management with e-signatures

Pricing

  • Starter: $29/month – 1 user, 1,500 contacts
  • Professional: $49/month – 3 users, 5,000 contacts
  • Team: $79/month – 5 users, unlimited contacts

Pros

  • Excellent communication tools
  • Easy-to-use interface
  • Good for teams that prioritize follow-up

Cons

  • Limited property management depth compared to AppFolio
  • No native accounting features

Recommendation

Best for agents who want to automate their lead follow-up and use video to stand out. Pair it with a separate accounting tool for full property management.

5. Propertyware – Best for Single-Family and Small Portfolio Management

Propertyware, owned by RealPage, is a cloud-based property management software tailored for single-family homes and small to mid-sized portfolios. It's less about sales and more about operational efficiency.

Key Features for Property Management

  • Owner and tenant portals
  • Work order management with mobile access
  • Online rent payments and lease tracking
  • Customizable lease templates
  • Financial reporting (balance sheets, P&L)

Pricing

  • Starts at $1 per unit per month (minimum $250/month)
  • Additional fees for premium support and training

Pros

  • Affordable per-unit pricing

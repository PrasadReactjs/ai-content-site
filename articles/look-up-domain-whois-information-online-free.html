How to look up domain WHOIS information online free — complete guide

Every domain name has a public registry record called WHOIS. It shows who owns a domain, when it was registered, when it expires, and which nameservers point it to a website. Whether you're researching a competitor, checking domain availability, or verifying your own domain details, you need a reliable way to look up domain WHOIS information online free. This page combines a live lookup tool with expert knowledge so you can get the full picture.

What is WHOIS and why does it matter?

WHOIS (pronounced "who is") is a query and response protocol that stores registration details for every domain. ICANN requires accredited registrars to maintain accurate WHOIS data. A typical record includes:

  • Registrar – the company where the domain was bought (e.g., Namecheap, GoDaddy).
  • Registrant name & organization – the owner (may be hidden via privacy).
  • Creation & expiry dates – critical for renewal planning.
  • Name servers – where the domain's DNS is managed.
  • Status codes

1. HubSpot CRM – Best Free Small Business CRM

Pricing: Free forever (up to 1,000 contacts) | Starter: $45/mo | Professional: $450/mo

Features

  • Contact management with deal tracking
  • Email tracking & templates
  • Meeting scheduler
  • Live chat & chatbot (free)
  • Basic reporting dashboard
  • Integrations with Gmail, Outlook, Slack, and 500+ apps

Pros

  • Generous free plan—no time limit
  • Intuitive interface, easy for non-techies
  • Excellent marketing automation add-ons
  • Huge library of free training resources

Cons

  • Paid tiers get expensive quickly
  • Limited customization on free plan
  • Some advanced features locked behind higher plans

Best for: Small businesses that want a powerful free CRM with room to grow into marketing and sales automation.
